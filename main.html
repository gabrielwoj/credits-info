Information
This page contains information about used profile pictures, sprays, etc., that are based on fan-artwork, and that I may use on Steam and other websites. This page is meant as a reference only.
The profile pictures themselves won't be hosted here, and only a link to the official Pixiv submission (or other official sources) will be shown (alongside character / franchise info, and artist's name). No booru links.
This is in case an artist decides to private or delete their artwork, and not have a mirror uploaded by me (despite being cropped) without their permission.
The profile pictures in this list only includes those who I have explicit permission from the artist to use, those that are tagged as "free icon" on Pixiv, art created by me, and those who are freely permitted based on an artist's Pixiv bio.
The "free icon" usage will be based on guidelines (if added) posted on the submission. Due to the language barrier, I'll be using Google Translate to get a rough idea about an artist's bio's information, as well "freeicon" usage.
A note will be given depending on each entry.
This list only applies to fan-made artwork, and not artwork that I classify as "official", as explained on my Steam Profile.
